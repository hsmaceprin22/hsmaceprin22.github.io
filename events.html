Project Launch Meeting

23-26 January 2024 Palazzo Giusso, Naples

About this Milestone

The kick-off meeting marked the official beginning of the H-SMA-CE project. The research teams from the University of Messina (RU1) and the University of Naples "L'Orientale" (RU2) gathered at Palazzo Giusso for three intensive days of planning and coordination.

Key Activities

  • Presentation of research objectives and expected outcomes
  • Definition of work packages and task assignments
  • Introduction of team members and role assignments
  • Planning the first field visit to Taurasi
  • Agreement on communication protocols and reporting structure
  • Definition of recruitment procedures
  • Journal Special Issue planning
  • Identification of relevant Conferences for dissemination of intermediate results

Project Progress Status: A Decision Support System for Circular Economy Transition

29-31 January 2025 Taurasi (AV) & Naples

About this Milestone

A comprehensive three-day research mission bringing together the H-SMA-CE project team with local stakeholders and municipal representatives to advance the circular economy transition framework for Historical Small Towns.

Programme

  • Wednesday, 29 January
    Workshop on project progress status at Palazzo Giusso, Naples. Presentations by Prof. Arbolino, Prof. Ioppolo, and Prof. Lopes with participation from Taurasi municipal representatives.

    Field visit to Taurasi. Meeting with municipal administration to discuss circular economy implementation strategies and data collection activities.
  • Friday, 31 January
    Follow-up workshop at Palazzo Giusso, Naples. Discussion of findings from Taurasi visit and planning of next research phases.
